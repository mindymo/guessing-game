This is a simple version of 21.

To begin, I will give you two cards. If you wish, you can draw one additional card.

If the total value of your cards is between 18 and 21, you win. But if the total value of your cards is lower than 18 or higher than 21, you lose!

Card values count upward from Ace (1 point) to Jack, Queen, and King (each 10 points).
