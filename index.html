Welcome, Player!

This is a simple game of 21.

To begin, I will give you two cards. You can draw up to four additional cards. If the total value of your cards is between 19 and 21, you win. But if the total face value of your cards exceeds 21, you lose!

Card values count upward from Ace (1 point) to Jack, Queen, and King (each 10 points).

Are you ready to play?
